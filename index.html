Horizons of Quantum Complexity
August 4, 2020
1:30PM - 4:00PM EDT (online)

Welcome to the John Templeton Symposium, "Horizons of Quantum Complexity," which will bring together diverse perpectives on fundamental problems in quantum many-body physics to cross-fertilize ideas and identify new research opportunities. During this 2.5-hour symposium session (online), each of the 11 invited panelists will present brief remarks highlighting a critical open problem or casting a vision for future research. A moderator will facilitate discussions stimulated by these remarks. The panelists are leaders from various sub-fields of theoretical and experimental physics including quantum simulation, condensed-matter physics, quantum information, quantum gravity, and chaos theory. The goal of this symposium is to explore the possibility of creating a program funded by Templeton Foundation that would address foundational questions in physics by bringing together the perspectives of different sub-disciplines.
